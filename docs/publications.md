## 2026

### Assessing vertical coordinate system performance in the Regional Modular Ocean Model 6 configuration for Northwest Pacific
**Authors**  
Inseong Chang, Young Ho Kim, Young-Gyu Park, Hyunkeun Jin, Gyundo Pak, Andrew C. Ross, Robert Hallberg

**Journal**  
*Geoscientific Model Development*, **19**, 187–216 (2026)

**DOI**  
`10.5194/gmd-19-187-2026`

**Keywords**  
MOM6, vertical coordinate system, HYBRID, ZSTAR, Northwest Pacific, regional ocean modeling

**Summary**  
This study evaluates HYBRID and ZSTAR vertical coordinate configurations in a MOM6-based regional ocean model for the Northwest Pacific.  
북서태평양 지역 해양모델 MOM6에서 HYBRID와 ZSTAR 수직 좌표계를 비교·평가한 연구이다.

## 2024

### Assessment of high-resolution regional ocean reanalysis K-ORA22 for the Northwest Pacific
**Authors**  
Inseong Chang, Young Ho Kim, Young-Gyu Park, Hyunkeun Jin, Gyundo Pak, Jae-Il Kwon, You-Soon Chang

**Journal**  
*Progress in Oceanography*, **229**, 103359 (2024)

**DOI**  
`10.1016/j.pocean.2024.103359`

**Keywords**  
K-ORA22, ocean reanalysis, KOOS-OPEM, Northwest Pacific, Korean marginal seas

북서태평양 및 한국 주변해를 위한 고해상도 지역 해양 재분석 K-ORA22를 제시하고 평가한 연구이다.

## 2023

### Impact of satellite and regional in-situ profile data assimilation on a high-resolution ocean prediction system in the Northwest Pacific
**Authors**  
Inseong Chang, Young Ho Kim, Hyunkeun Jin, Young-Gyu Park, Gyundo Pak, You-Soon Chang

**Journal**  
*Frontiers in Marine Science*, **10**, 1085542 (2023)

**DOI**  
`10.3389/fmars.2023.1085542`

**Keywords**  
data assimilation, EnOI, satellite altimetry, KOOS-OPEM, regional observing networks

**Summary**  
This paper examines how satellite observations and regional in-situ profile data improve a high-resolution ocean prediction system in the Northwest Pacific.  
위성 관측과 지역 현장관측 자료가 고해상도 북서태평양 해양 예측시스템 개선에 어떻게 기여하는지를 평가한 연구이다.
